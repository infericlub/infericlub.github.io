inferi is a group of friends writing about computer science and information security, rootkits, reverse engineering, web exploitation, mobile, Active Directory, kernel internals, and assorted curiosities that don't fit anywhere else.

Each post is signed by whoever wrote it. We don't agree on everything; we share a desk.
